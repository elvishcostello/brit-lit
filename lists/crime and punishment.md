Excellent category! Here are more British crime and punishment terms:

**Legal Profession:**

- Barrister (courtroom advocate)
- Solicitor (general legal work, prepares cases)
- KC/QC (King's/Queen's Counsel - senior barrister who "takes silk")
- Chambers (barrister's office)
- Bencher (senior member of Inn of Court)
- Clerk (barrister's clerk - manages cases/fees)
- Brief (legal case documents given to barrister)

**Court Terms:**

- Magistrate (local judge for minor crimes)
- Assizes (periodic criminal courts in counties)
- Sessions/Quarter Sessions
- The dock (where accused stands)
- The bar (barrier in court)
- Witness box
- The bench (judges' seat)

**Police/Criminal Slang:**

- Peeler/Bobby (policeman)
- Rozzer (police)
- The nick (police station; also "to nick" = arrest)
- Nicked/pinched (arrested)
- Lag/lagged (convict/sentenced to penal servitude)
- Screwing (burglary)
- Cracksman (safecracker)
- Area sneak (burglar through servants' entrance)
- Magsman (confidence trickster)
- Fence (receiver of stolen goods)

**Punishment:**

- Transportation (exile to Australia)
- Penal servitude
- Hard labour
- The Hulks (prison ships)
- Treadmill (prison punishment)
- The birch/birching (corporal punishment)
- Tyburn (historic execution site)
- Condemned cell
- The drop (gallows trapdoor)

Ah yes! Specific British prisons and criminal justice locations:

**London Prisons:**

- Newgate (already mentioned - most notorious, demolished 1902)
- Holloway (women's prison)
- Pentonville
- Wormwood Scrubs (The Scrubs)
- Brixton
- Wandsworth
- The Marshalsea (debtors' prison - Dickens's father was there)
- Fleet Prison (debtors')
- Millbank (where transportation began)
- Coldbath Fields

**Outside London:**

- Dartmoor (remote moorland prison)
- Portland (on Isle of Portland)
- Parkhurst (Isle of Wight)
- Reading Gaol (Oscar Wilde imprisoned there)

**Special Institutions:**

- Broadmoor (criminal lunatic asylum)
- Rampton (criminal lunatic asylum)

**Courts/Police:**

- Bow Street (Bow Street Runners, magistrates court)
- Marlborough Street (magistrates court)

**Transportation Destinations:**

- Botany Bay (Australia - generic term)
- Van Diemen's Land (Tasmania)
- Norfolk Island (harshest penal colony)

**Execution Sites:**

- Tyburn Tree (historic, near Marble Arch)
- Execution Dock (Wapping - for pirates/maritime crimes)
